Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

At MyLiveTV, we are committed to providing premium free content while advancing streaming technology through research.

1. Data Collection & Permissions

To support our free service model and improve user experience, we request access to:

  • Microphone & Camera: Used to analyze environmental audio/visual feedback during streaming for quality optimization.
  • Location Services: Used to understand regional content demand and network performance.
  • File Access: Used for temporary caching to ensure smooth, buffer-free playback.

2. Data Security & Research Ethics

All data collected is anonymized and encrypted. We do not link this data to your personal identity. We guarantee that your data will not be leaked, sold to third-party advertisers, or used for any purpose outside of our internal research framework.

3. Your Consent

By using MyLiveTV, you explicitly consent to this data collection. You can revoke permissions via your device settings at any time, though some features may become unavailable.
